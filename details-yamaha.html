Yamaha YZF-R1

The Yamaha YZF-R1 is a high-performance sportbike renowned for its powerful 998cc engine and cutting-edge technology.

Specification Detail
Engine 998cc inline-four, liquid-cooled, DOHC
Power 200 hp @ 13,500 rpm
Torque 112.4 Nm @ 11,500 rpm
Transmission 6-speed constant mesh
Suspension (Front) 43mm KYB inverted fork, fully adjustable
Suspension (Rear) KYB monoshock, fully adjustable
Brakes (Front) Dual 320mm disc, dual radial-mount calipers
Brakes (Rear) 220mm disc, single caliper
Wheelbase 1,405 mm
Seat Height 855 mm
Weight 200 kg (wet)
Price $16,500
