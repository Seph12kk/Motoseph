Suzuki Raider 150 FI

The Suzuki Raider 150 FI is a powerful and nimble bike with a 147.3cc engine. It's known for its agility and performance in urban environments.

Specification Detail
Engine 147.3cc single-cylinder, liquid-cooled
Power 18.4 hp @ 10,500 rpm
Torque 13.8 Nm @ 8,500 rpm
Transmission 6-speed constant mesh
Suspension (Front) Upside-down fork
Suspension (Rear) Mono shock
Brakes (Front) Disc, double-piston caliper
Brakes (Rear) Disc, single-piston caliper
Wheelbase 1,300 mm
Seat Height 780 mm
Weight 115 kg (wet)
