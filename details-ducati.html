Ducati Panigale V4

The Ducati Panigale V4 is a high-performance superbike with a 1,103cc V4 engine. It combines advanced aerodynamics and electronics for an exhilarating riding experience.

Specification Detail
Engine 1,103cc V4, liquid-cooled
Power 214 hp @ 13,000 rpm
Torque 124 Nm @ 10,000 rpm
Transmission 6-speed constant mesh
Suspension (Front) 43mm Öhlins NIX30, fully adjustable
Suspension (Rear) Öhlins TTX36, fully adjustable
Brakes (Front) 330mm dual discs, Brembo calipers
Brakes (Rear) 245mm disc, single caliper
Wheelbase 1,460 mm
Seat Height 830 mm
Weight 198 kg (wet)
Price $25,000
