Kawasaki Ninja ZX-6R

The Kawasaki Ninja ZX-6R is a superb sportbike with an aggressive design and a potent 636cc engine. It offers an exhilarating ride and cutting-edge technology.

Specification Detail
Engine 636cc inline-four, liquid-cooled
Power 130 hp @ 13,000 rpm
Torque 70 Nm @ 11,500 rpm
Transmission 6-speed constant mesh
Suspension (Front) 41mm inverted fork, fully adjustable
Suspension (Rear) Uni-Trak monoshock, fully adjustable
Brakes (Front) 310mm dual discs, dual radial-mount calipers
Brakes (Rear) 220mm disc, single caliper
Wheelbase 1,405 mm
Seat Height 825 mm
Weight 194 kg (wet)
Price $10,500
