Honda CBR500R

The Honda CBR500R is a sleek and sporty motorcycle designed for both new and experienced riders. Its 471cc engine offers a perfect balance of performance and efficiency.

Specification Detail
Engine 471cc parallel-twin, liquid-cooled
Power 47 hp @ 8,600 rpm
Torque 43 Nm @ 6,500 rpm
Transmission 6-speed constant mesh
Suspension (Front) 41mm telescopic fork
Suspension (Rear) Pro-Link monoshock
Brakes (Front) 310mm disc, dual-piston caliper
Brakes (Rear) 240mm disc, single-piston caliper
Wheelbase 1,410 mm
Seat Height 790 mm
Weight 192 kg (wet)
Price $7,500
